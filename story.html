Our point of view

We are in the discovery business.

SIFTED exists for pieces that still have life, beauty, usefulness, and story left in them.

One person’s moved-on-from can become another person’s favorite find.

We believe remarkable things deserve remarkable homes. Some pieces are new, some are gently used, and some are beautifully aged. What matters is whether they carry presence, quality, and feeling.

SIFTED is intentionally not a crowded marketplace. It is a curated home for pieces chosen slowly and presented honestly.
